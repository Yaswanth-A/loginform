About Us

IIT Madras, the home to some of the best brains in the country is also home to several top notch athletes. With the best infrastructure facilities available on campus, IIT Madras boasts a sports culture that is of quite a high standard. An Olympic size swimming pool, the impressive IIT-Chemplast cricket ground, a vast stadium and a hi-tech wood-floored badminton court are all significant nodes of the exuberant sports culture here, in addition to the many grounds and fields within and outside hostels. There are various sports events taking place throughout the year including intra-hostel events, Schroeter- the Inter-hostel tournament, Agrata-the inter-collegiate tournament hosted by IIT Madras, and the ultimate Inter IIT Sports Meet which is held annually in rotation by the older IITs.
